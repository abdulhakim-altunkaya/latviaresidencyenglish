Developed EU Country

Latvia, a member of the European Union and NATO since 2004, is also part of the Schengen and Eurozone areas.

Schengen

With residence card from Latvia, you can freely travel to all EU member states, as well as Norway, Switzerland, and Iceland.

High Quality Education

Latvia provides quality education. Your children can attend public schools free of charge, and Latvian diplomas are recognized in Europe.

Your Children

After 10 years, you can obtain Latvian citizenship and grant your spouse and children a second citizenship.

Safe Country

Latvia is one of the safest countries in Europe and the world. Your children can walk to and from school on their own.

Natural Beauty

Latvia is surrounded by forests, lakes, rivers and pristine beaches, and has preserved its natural structure.

European Market

A secure gateway, Latvia opens doors to all EU countries for your investment. By establishing a company here, you can trade freely with EU member states.

Quality of Life

Latvia has a high quality of life, with parks everywhere. There is little traffic, clean air, and people are tolerant and respectful of each other.

Zero Stray Dogs

There are no stray dogs or cats in Latvia, and there are no diseases on the streets. Your children can play safely on the streets.
